01 About Me

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast focused on offensive security and penetration testing. With a background in IT support and systems administration, I made the move into security because I love understanding how systems break and how to fix them.

I spend my time working through HackTheBox and TryHackMe machines, building custom recon tools in Python, documenting everything I learn, and studying for certs. This site is my public knowledge base and portfolio.
